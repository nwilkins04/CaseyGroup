Leadership

The Casey Group executive team brings unsurpasses business and technology expertise to your project. The professional, innovative culture, derived from the top leaders, is evident in every Casey employee.
Rich Casey, Chairman of the board and CEO
Rich Casey is the chairman of the board and CEo of the Casey Group. He founded the company in 1989 with the mission of leveraging information technology to deliver strategic advantage and operational efficiencies to his clients.
Prior to founding The Casey Group, Rich held various positions in the IT industry, begining his career at AT&T in 1980. In 2002, he was awarded the Small Business Entrepreneur of the Year Award by the Morris County Chamber of Commerce (NJ).
Rich holds an MBA in Finance from Fairleigh Dickinson University and a Bachelor's degree in Biology from Rider University. He also completed a three-year executive management program for owners, presidents and managers and attended the Advanced Learning Institute at the Harvard Business School.
In addition to his leadership of The Casey Group, Rich is the former Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Affinity Federal Credit Union, the largest credit union in New Jersey.
Laura A. DeRosa, Director of Application Development and Support
Laura is responsible for managing multiple client projects and new client engagements. Key clients include Tyco and ADT. Laura has been directing the teams responsile for new development and production support for these clients and others for over 11 years. Her teams are skilled across multiple technologies with deep experience in mainframe and web technologies. Laura has been in the IT business for over 30 years. Prior to joining The Casey Group in 2003, Laura had over 20 years of experience working with large IT Service provider companies like Prudential Financial Services, ADP, Deloitte Consulting, Dun and Bradstreet, and The Cubb Institute.
Throughout her career, Laura has demonstrated the ability to lead and manage large distributed teams, located onsite, onshore, and offshore, in the successful completion of complex projects.
