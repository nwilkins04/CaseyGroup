Service: Custom Application Development

The Challenge

The client offers a fully managed storage and delivery service for owners and distributors of digital content to movie theaters and other venues. Supported by its robust platform of fail-safe internet data centers, the company is able to leverage the market-leading rold of its hollywood software subsidiary with the innovative digital delivery capapbilies of its digital media unit to provide the highest level of technology avalible to service the emerging digital cinema industry.
When the client was founded, the entertainment industry was only begining to grasp the technologies and standards for digital cinema, the client wanted to gain early entry into this new delivery medium and to help shape the standards. However, it did not have the technology resources to create the software it needed.

The Solution

The client selected The Casey Group for its custom application development and we architected and implemented the prototype for a digital media management system that processes digital flim from the point of ingestion, into the data center and then on through the delivery of the flim to the exhibitors via satelite. The project was performed in two phases. The first phase was the initial development for the proof of conect. In this phase the infrastructure was built out as well as the initial devices selected for reciving the delivered digital content. The proof of concept demonstrated the ability to recieve the digitial content in the proposed industry format and then manage the distribution to multiple exhibitors. The second phase expanded the work of the first phase, which entailed developing the lower level detail of managing the DCP (Digital Cinema Package) files. In this phase, The Casey Group also introduce an automated satelite transmission for distribution, nuilt a management server for housing the DCP essence files and built generic management process for creating scheudles and playlists. The project entailed:

  • Modeling and architecture of all components
  • Converting the original proof of concept software from a Windows platform to a true corss pltform system. (Windows, Unix, Linux)
  • Data modeling
  • Integration of satelite software with the application
  • Tuning and benchmarking of satelite software
  • Interpreting constantly evolving industry specifications into code
  • GUI interfaces for management software and theatre file management
  • Implementation and testing on remote distribution NOC
  • Development of a propriety notification system
  • Development of all code

The Impact

The Casey Group successfully met the client's objectives and enabled the company to successfully enter the market. As a result, it quickly became a leader in the digital cinema distribution channel and today is the recognized leader in transforming movie theatres into digital and networked entertainment centers.
