Fire and Security Firm (Fortune 500)
Service: Custom Application Development

The Challenge

A leading fire and security firm that provides monitoring systems for the commercial and residential business markets was managing its documents manually which was intensive, error prone, costly, and exceedingly slow in document turn around. Also, to reduce costs and gain improvised operational efficiencies, the company wanted to consolidate its operations.

The Solution

The company chose our custom application development servce to develop its document management system. Based on the client's specifications and out IT assessment, we developed solutions that resulted in significant improvements in its document management processes.
The client's document management system was part of a four year order to cash re-engineering program that the company had engaged The Casey Group to lead. For the document management system, The Casey Group worked closely with the client in devising the automated scanning and workflow processes for handling the multitude of contracts and order documents. The design called for the information to be maintained in a content management database where it is electroncially stored and can be searched and retrieved as required. Additionally, the work entailed defining and designing the automated process.

The Impact

With the help of The Casey Group, the documents now move through a systematic workflow that

  • Eliminates paper handling of the documnets
  • Reduces errors
  • Provides routing based on business rules
  • Automates escalations for review and approval
  • Reduces overall processing time
  • Allowed processing to be centralized
  • Integrates with other systems that rely on the documents and real time processing status
The system went live in early 2010 in a limited prototype rollout. It has now been expanded into all of the company's branch offices.

The Challenge

Our client is a major distributor and retailor of automotive parts. The company had begun an initiative to modernize and re-write the current application system to improve its overall performances and functionality. In order to do this, a decision was made to free up internal IT resources from maintaining and enhancing the existing system while the new development was in progress. Additionally, IT resources were to be freed up from normal day to day production bug fixing on the exisiting system.

The Solution

To enable it to focus on its new development efforts, the client selected The Casey Group to manage some of their IT systems.
The Casey Group wastasksed with performing all system analysis, application development and production break fix. Part of the challenge was working with older technologies that were in use as well as having to become familiar with the client's internal code and business processes in a short period of time.
To help achieve this and to meet all requirements, The Casey Group's development team did a series of reverse engineering on the application using various tools and techniques. A mock production application was then setup to simulate the production environment an associated connections. The result of this process was extremly positive. The developers were able to respond within the agreed upen SLA's for production issues as well as respond to the various enhancement requests that were bring made by the business.

The Impact

The Casey Group's support of the client's legacy system ultimately allowed the company to successfully develop its new system and begin to rollout according to their growther plans.
